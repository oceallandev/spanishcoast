FAQ

Do I need expensive devices?

No. The right setup is usually a small set of reliable devices, not a huge smart-home project.

Can you manage codes for guests?

Yes. For managed properties we can set and rotate access codes as part of operations.

Can this help business transfers?

Yes. In traspaso deals, access systems and tech accounts are often where handovers fail.
