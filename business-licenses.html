Business Licences & Permits

A business can look perfect on paper, but you still need to be able to operate legally. We help clients obtain or transfer the right licences and permits in Costa Blanca South by coordinating with architects and specialist professionals, and by keeping paperwork and next steps structured.

Names and requirements vary by town and activity, but the goal is always the same: ensure you can open, trade, and operate without hidden blockers.

What We Do

We are not an architecture studio and we are not a law firm. Our role is to keep the process moving and coordinated: the right checks, the right documents, and the right specialists at the right time.

Feasibility check

Confirm the activity fits the premises and local rules (before you commit time and money).

Architect coordination

We work with architects for measurements, technical documents, and submission steps.

Checklist & timeline

Clear next steps, dependencies, and a realistic time-to-open plan.

Follow-ups

Keep communication tight so the process does not stall in “waiting for a reply”.

Traspaso vs New Opening

Traspaso (existing business)

We verify licence status, what can be transferred, and what must be updated for the new operator.

New opening (new activity)

We coordinate feasibility checks and the steps required to open legally from scratch.

Premises changes

If works are needed, we coordinate scope and sequence so it does not break your launch timeline.

The most common mistake is assuming a licence “comes with the business”. We help you confirm the reality early, before the deal becomes complicated.

FAQ

How long does it take?

It depends on town and activity. We give a realistic timeline once we know the premises and the activity.

Do you guarantee approval?

No. Approvals depend on the Ayuntamiento and compliance. Our job is to reduce risk and keep the process clean.

Can you check before I buy?

Yes, and you should. A fast feasibility check early can save months later.
