How The Process Works

New builds are often faster than resale once you have the right development, but they have extra “hidden” steps. We make the sequence explicit so nothing gets missed.

1) Requirements

Budget, towns, delivery timing, and must-haves (terrace, pool, walk-to-beach, parking).

2) Shortlist + comparisons

We compare developments side-by-side: location, build spec, and what is included.

3) Viewings

We plan viewings so you can compare properly and avoid “sales-tour fatigue”.

4) Reservation

Support on next steps and coordination with the right licensed professionals when required.

5) Build timeline

Milestones, handover expectations, and any practical planning you need around completion.

6) Snagging & keys

Final checks, punch list, and a clean handover so you can start using the property.

Note: we are not a law firm. Where formal legal representation is required, we coordinate with licensed professionals.
