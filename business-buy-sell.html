Entrepreneurs relocating to Spain, investors looking for proven cash-flow, and owners who want a clean exit without constant back-and-forth.

FAQ

What is “traspaso”?

A transfer of a running business (goodwill and equipment) under a lease. Terms vary by deal.

Can you shortlist options?

Yes. Send your sector, area, and budget, and we will shortlist realistic opportunities.

Do you handle contracts?

We support the process with in-house basic legal help and coordinate specialist lawyers when needed.
