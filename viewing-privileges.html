New Builds Privileges

New build buyers receive two completion credits: a Viewing Trip Credit and a Return Stay Credit. Caps are tied to the purchase price to stay proportional for different budgets. The Return Stay Credit can be used for a second visit here, or as a travel credit elsewhere.

€250k – €399k

Viewing Trip Credit up to €1,500 + Return Stay Credit up to €750.

€400k – €699k

Viewing Trip Credit up to €2,500 + Return Stay Credit up to €1,000.

€700k – €999k

Viewing Trip Credit up to €3,500 + Return Stay Credit up to €1,250.

€1,000,000+

Viewing Trip Credit up to €5,000 + Return Stay Credit up to €1,500.

Viewing Trip Credit is receipt-based and paid at completion (not at reservation). Return Stay Credit is issued at completion as a travel credit/voucher (no cash alternative; valid for 12 months). One buying party per purchase.

Resale Privileges

For resales, we run a monthly VIP credit during March and April. This is designed to create urgency without turning the market into a discount race.

Monthly VIP Viewing Trip Credit

In March 2026 and April 2026, the first resale buyer to sign a binding private purchase contract receives a Viewing Trip Credit up to €1,500, paid at completion.

Completion Concierge Pack

All resale buyers who sign during the campaign window receive structured completion support: timeline, notary coordination, and handover planning.

VIP selection is based on timestamp of signature recorded by Spanish Coast Properties. If the VIP buyer cancels or becomes ineligible, the next eligible buyer in that month is selected.

How It Works

1) Book a structured viewing trip

We confirm your budget and must-haves, then build a shortlist and itinerary so the trip stays tight.

2) Reserve during the campaign window

To qualify, you must sign a binding reservation/private contract between Mar 1 and Apr 30, 2026.

3) Complete and receive credits

At notary completion, we process your eligible credits (up to caps) against receipts.

Key Terms (Short Version)

  • Credits are paid at completion only (not at reservation).
  • Viewing Trip Credit is receipt-based: flights, accommodation, car hire, and transfers. Excludes meals, upgrades, and non-viewing excursions.
  • Return Stay Credit is issued at completion as a travel credit/voucher (no cash alternative; valid for 12 months), for a second visit here or elsewhere. One buying party per purchase. Caps apply by tier (new builds) and monthly VIP cap applies for resales.
  • Campaign window: Mar 1 to Apr 30, 2026 (CET/CEST). Completion may occur after the window.
  • Limited availability and may end early if capacity is reached. Terms apply.
