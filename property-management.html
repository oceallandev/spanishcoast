What Management Can Include

Keyholding

Secure spare keys and controlled access when trades or guests need entry.

Check-ins & check-outs

Practical handover, basic inspection, and issue reporting with photo evidence.

Routine checks

Periodic checks to catch water leaks, electricity issues, or damage early.

Maintenance coordination

Quotes, scheduling, and updates. You approve, we execute and report.

Scope depends on your property and needs. Some owners want minimal keyholding, others want full coordination.

How Onboarding Works

1) Setup call

Confirm property details, access points, and the exact responsibilities we take on.

2) Key handover

Secure keys, document what we received, and confirm emergency contacts.

3) First inspection

Baseline checks so we know what “normal” is and what needs attention.

4) Ongoing updates

Clear updates after events: check-ins, maintenance visits, and inspections.
