Legal Advice (Business Transfers)

We are not a law firm. We provide in-house basic legal support for business operations (practical legal guidance and contract drafting, led by a University graduate in Law and Administrative Sciences), and we coordinate the right licensed professionals when a matter requires specialist representation or formal legal sign-off.

For traspaso deals, the most common risk is assuming something “comes with the business” when it does not. We help you verify the key facts so your legal team can work cleanly.

Many transfers also depend on technology: POS systems, CCTV, alarm panels, WiFi, booking tools, and smart devices. We bring hands-on software and hardware knowledge so the handover includes the real items that keep a business running, not just the keys.
