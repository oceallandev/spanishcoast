## Offer Summary

- New builds (buyers): tiered Viewing Trip Credit (up to €5,000) + Return Stay Credit (up to €1,500), paid at completion.
- New builds (buyers): tiered Viewing Trip Credit (up to €5,000) + Return Stay Credit (up to €1,500) issued at completion as a travel credit/voucher (no cash alternative; valid 12 months).
- Resales (buyers): monthly VIP (March 2026 + April 2026): first binding private purchase contract each month receives Viewing Trip Credit up to €1,500, paid at completion.
- Resales (buyers): all who sign within the campaign window receive the Completion Concierge Pack (operational support, non-monetary).
