How It Works

1) Take a photo

Capture the “For Sale” board clearly (phone number visible).

2) Send location

We attach GPS so we know the exact street and can react fast.

3) We call and onboard

We contact the owner/agency, verify details, and add it to our portfolio.

4) You get rewarded

When the deal closes, you receive a minimum €200 or €500 depending on the property tier.

Note: payouts are subject to verification (duplicate submissions, fake boards, wrong location, or non-onboarded listings are not eligible).
