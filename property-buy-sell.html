How It Works

1) Requirements

Budget, towns, property type, and the “must-haves” that actually decide the purchase.

2) Shortlist

We reduce noise and focus only on realistic options worth your time (and your trip).

3) Viewings

A structured plan so you can compare properly and move fast when you find the right fit.

4) Offer to keys

Negotiation support and coordination of next steps through reservation and completion.
